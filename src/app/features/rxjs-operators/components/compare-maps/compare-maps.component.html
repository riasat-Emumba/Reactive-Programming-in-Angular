Conclusion

In summary, each map operator in RxJS has its unique behavior:

  • Map: Transforms each value emitted by the source observable.
  • MergeMap: Flattens the observables and does not preserve the order of emitted values.
  • ConcatMap: Flattens the observables while preserving the order of emitted values.
  • SwitchMap: Switches to a new observable on each emission, cancelling the previous one.

Choose the appropriate operator based on the specific requirements of your data flow and the desired order of emissions.
